Section 2: introduction

The Jedi Temple Guards are elite members of the Jedi Order whose purpose is to defend the temple and the Jedi within it at all costs. They are highly skilled and disciplined, being feared by Sith and anyone who should oppose them. Without the Jedi Temple Guards, the Jedi temple would be left undefended.

Section 3: rules & regulations

 

1: All Temple Guards ranking Temple Guard Lieutenant and below have an OOS, Oath of Silence. Breaking this will result in punishments.

2: Listen to TG Superiors.

3: Follow your duties at all times, unless told otherwise by Captain+

4: Be mature at all times.

5: Check this regularly.

Section 4: Ranks

 

Chief of security

Commander of TG, leader of the division.


Commander

This person is to make sure all  Guards and all Guard Enforcers are doing their jobs correctly

 

Captain

3rd in command of TG.

lieutenant

Third HR rank.

 

Sentry

Second HR rank.

 

Instructor

First HR rank.

 

Senior guardian
The rank that is considered to be non commissioned and people who help officers with training and tryouts.

Temple Guardian
A very experienced temple guardian, the main force of the TG.

Junior Guardian
A temple guardian with a little more experience than Novitiate.

Novitate

The base rank for all members who are active enough to get promoted from candidate

Candidate

Training rank.

Section 5: duties

 

The normal duties are to protect the Temple at all costs from hostiles, if you’re in a subdivision, that’s your job.

Section 6: formation & commands

 

Commands can only be said by Instructor+
[+] Ignite your saber
[-] Disignite your saber
[EX-(player)] (ex. [EX-IcyN1c0] Kill the Target, no hesitation
[KZA] Killzone activated
[KZD] Killzone deactivated
[D] Disengage: Cancel all Commands previously given
[DW] Defensive Wall (STS in front of the person and look at the same direction he looks at)
[G] Guard Command (ex. [G-IcyN1c0]
[SO] Spread out (previous command but move more far)
[TU] Tighten up ( previous command but move closer)
[STS] Shoulder to Shoulder

[FORMATIONS]

[W] Wedge
[FW] Front Wedge
[RW] Right Wing.
[LW] Left Wing
[B] Box
[SFL] Single File Line
[Wall]
[F.Wall] Front Wall
[DW] Defensive Wall
[IW] The inverted formation of the previous command (ex. wedge would be inverted wedge)

Section 4: Combat training
This training is to test and improve the TG’s combat skills, It involves 2 1v1’s, 1 2v2 (If there are enough members), 1 round of HR and finally 1 round FFA

Section 5: law training
Law Training goes over the laws and tests the attendees understanding Of the TJO’s law, it includes hypothetical situations ex.(What would you do if a Force sensitive jumped on your head?)In which the attendees must answer 5 questions correctly, It also involves 5 questions from the TG handbook.

Section 2: low ranks
               

 Candidate: To rank up from candidate you have to pass an induction training hosted by the TG High Rank

Novitiate: To rank up from Novitiate you have  to pass 1 Combat Training, 1 Law training and finally 1 Formation training.

Junior Guardian: To rank up from Junior Guardian you have to pass 2 of each training, that includes 2 law trainings, 2 combat trainings and lastly 2 formation trainings.

Section 3: Medium ranks


Temple Guardian: To rank up from Temple Guardian you have to log a total of 4 hours in guard logs and also attend 2 patrols.

 

Senior Guardian: To Rank up from Senior Guardian you need to be active and showcase that you have the ability and dedication to get a HR rank, you must also attend 2 patrols.

Section 4: High Ranks

Instructor: To obtain Instructor you need to be active and showcase that you have the ability and dedication to get a HR rank.

Sentry: To obtain Sentry you must show your activity by hosting events for the TG, also you may assist higher ups.

Lieutenant: To obtain Lieutenant you must get handpicked by the commander.

Captain: To obtain  captain you must get handpicked by the Chief of security.

Commander: To obtain commander you must get handpicked by the Chief of security.


Chief of Security: To obtain Chief of Security you have to get handpicked by the TG overseer.
